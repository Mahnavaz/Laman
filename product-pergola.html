Pergola

A modular urban structure with minimal design, creating quality shelter and optimal shade for public spaces

Smart Shading for Urban Interactions

Laman Pergola is a modular urban structure that, with minimal design and the use of simple materials, creates quality shelter and optimal shade for public spaces. This product, as part of Laman's mission to enhance 'quality of staying', transforms the city into a space for presence and tranquility.

Flexibility in Dimensions and Form

The key feature of the pergola is its fully modular structure that gives designers great freedom of action: Variable Dimensions - the structure is designed so that its dimensions can be changed and customized according to project needs and surface coverage in different sizes. Variety in Form - these pieces, like smart puzzle pieces, have the ability to combine and expand to create diverse forms in urban open spaces.

Creating Dynamic Collective Space

The pergola goes beyond a simple shading function and acts as an architectural element: Social Interaction Platform - this structure can accommodate a dynamic and public collective space beneath it and provide a platform for forming direct interactions. Visual Quality Enhancement - in addition to shading functionality, this product contributes to the visual beauty of the environment with its minimal design and creates an identity-rich space.

Construction Engineering and Sustainability

Like all Laman products, the pergola is based on the design language of simplicity and efficiency: Industrial and Prefabricated Production - the production process of this structure is fully engineered, providing guaranteed construction precision and quality in the shortest time. Sustainable Architecture - this product is designed using minimal materials to leave the smallest environmental footprint on the urban environment.

Technical Specifications

Length 350 cm (customizable)
Width 300 cm (customizable)
Height 330 cm
Configuration Modular (expandable)
