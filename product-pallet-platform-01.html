Pallet Platform 01

A modular urban furniture system that transforms abandoned spaces into 'islands for pause and interaction'. Each module is designed exactly to the size of a car parking space, creating a human and lively environment in the heart of the city by liberating parking space. The engineered angular form provides a pleasant corner for rest, and with attachments like tables and plant boxes, it becomes an ideal platform for conversation or creating urban pop-up cafés.

Redefining Urban Space; From Parking to Living Space

In today's cities, where cars occupy a major portion of public spaces, Laman brand offers an architectural and human solution to return 'presence' to the city. The Pallet Platform with bench is not merely a piece of urban furniture; it is a tool for transforming forgotten and unused spaces into islands for pause, conversation, and urban life.

From Car Space to Human Territory

The dimensions of each Pallet Platform module are designed to fit exactly within a car parking space. This approach, rooted in the global Parklet concept, creates a human, lively, and interactive platform in the heart of the city by liberating space occupied by cars. The result is transforming a merely transit space into a destination for stopping, interaction, and collective experience.

Modular Structure; Freedom in Design

The Pallet Platform with bench is designed completely modular. Each module acts as an independent unit, but alongside other modules can create diverse arrangements; from a simple linear bench to large urban seating islands. The angular and engineered form of this system creates an enclosed, readable, and pleasant space that both provides a sense of security and gives identity to the surrounding context.

Minimal Aesthetics, Lasting Presence

Laman's design language is based on simplicity, readability, and avoiding exaggeration. The Pallet Platform with bench, with simple lines and honest materials, creates a calm and reliable corner in the city's visual chaos. This system can be placed along cafés, sidewalks, cultural spaces, or urban open areas and enhance the quality of staying for users.

Industrial Production; Precise, Fast, and Sustainable

All components of the Pallet Platform are produced prefabricated and industrially. This feature, in addition to ensuring quality and durability, provides the possibility of quick and easy installation with minimal site intervention. Built-in leveling bases in the structure allow precise adjustment on sloped or uneven surfaces, and the ability to disassemble and relocate makes this product an ideal option for temporary or seasonal projects.

Diverse Uses and Smart Attachments

The Pallet Platform with bench is a platform beyond simple seating. This system has the capability to be equipped with various attachments such as plant boxes, tables, and additional chairs. This flexibility provides the possibility of adapting the product to different urban needs and various usage scenarios; from brief daily pauses to forming meaningful social interactions.

Technical Specifications

Width 190 cm
