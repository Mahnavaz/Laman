Picnic Set

A prominent member of the modular urban furniture family, combining industrial strength with modern aesthetics

Sustainable Materials & Warmth

The strength of this product lies in the use of recycled railway sleeper wood for the table and bench surfaces, which, in addition to environmental sustainability, brings warmth and a unique texture to the cold urban space. These thick planks are mounted on hot-dip galvanized steel bases (with 50-year corrosion resistance) to ensure the final product maintains its durability and quality under the harshest weather conditions and continuous use.

Modular Design Philosophy

The picnic set is designed with a modular approach, allowing for flexible arrangements in various urban spaces. The unified design language ensures seamless integration with other products in the Laman City collection, creating cohesive outdoor environments.

Industrial Production & Quality

Manufactured using advanced industrial processes, each picnic set undergoes rigorous quality control to ensure consistency and longevity. The combination of recycled materials and modern manufacturing techniques results in a product that is both environmentally responsible and built to last.

Technical Specifications

Table Length 180 cm
Table Width 80 cm
Table Height 75 cm
Material Recycled Railway Sleeper Wood & Hot-Dip Galvanized Steel
