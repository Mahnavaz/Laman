Smart Guardian of Nature in Urban Architecture

Laman Tree Guard is a vital element with minimal design and robust steel structure that ensures the health of tree trunks and roots against damage from traffic and environmental factors. This product, by completely covering the soil surface and eliminating irregularities, levels the ground with the sidewalk surface and gives a neat and integrated appearance to the public space. As a result, this tool, in addition to preserving the city's natural assets, significantly contributes to spatial organization and enhancement of the environment's visual quality and prevents unwanted visual interventions.

Technical Specifications and Construction Standards

This product is designed according to Laman's engineered and prefabricated production process to provide high construction precision and verified durability in urban projects. The tree guard model has standard dimensions of 120 by 120 centimeters with a thickness of 5 centimeters, creating proportionate and precise coverage for the green space of sidewalks. The design of this element is based on Laman's design language of 'simplicity' and 'readability' to perform its protective function while having minimal visual intervention in the urban environment's appearance.

Benefits of Use in Urban Landscape Projects

Using Laman tree guards in squares and sidewalks is an architectural solution for enhancing 'quality of staying' and transforming transit points into organized and structured spaces. These pieces, by eliminating irregularities around the tree trunk, provide a safe and obstacle-free path for citizens' movement and prevent pollution accumulation around the roots. The product's steel structure has the necessary resistance against physical pressures in high-traffic environments and gives a modern visual identity to urban design projects.
