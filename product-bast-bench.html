Bast Bench

Bast is a symbol of concentrated strength in urban furniture. This model is constructed by compressing four thick recycled railway sleeper wood planks with metal fasteners, providing exceptional durability in the harshest environmental conditions. Each Bast unit, along with its metal bases, is considered a complete and independent module. The key feature of this product is its high flexibility in arrangement; modules can be placed linearly side by side or stacked in a stepped manner, creating diverse and multi-level seating platforms and stages suited to the topography and needs of public spaces.

Bast Bench: Symbol of Concentrated Strength and Sustainable Urban Architecture

Bast Bench is introduced as a symbol of power and density in urban furniture. This product, with a bold approach to sustainable architecture, breathes new life into resilient materials to provide an authentic foundation for citizens' presence and comfort.

Design Philosophy and Material: Railway Heritage in Service of the City

Bast Bench is entirely made from recycled and valuable materials that inject identity and resilience into urban space:

Intelligent use of railway sleeper wood: This product is constructed by compressing four thick recycled railway sleeper wood planks (beams used in railway tracks) with sturdy metal fasteners.

Exceptional durability: These planks, which have been under pressure and harsh weather conditions on railway tracks for years, guarantee the product's stability and longevity in the harshest environmental conditions.

Environmental approach: Reusing this authentic material leaves the smallest environmental footprint and creates a new life cycle for resilient wood.

Puzzle-like Arrangement and Creating Interactive Platforms

The unique feature of Bast Bench is its unparalleled flexibility in arrangement and modular capabilities:

Puzzle-like module connection: Each Bast unit, along with its metal bases, is considered a complete and independent module that can be combined with other units.

Creating platforms and stages: Modules are placed not only linearly but also in a puzzle-like and stepped manner on top of each other. This capability allows the creation of diverse and multi-level seating platforms suited to public space needs and ground topography.

Space for gathering: This multi-faceted structure transforms the bench into an attractive point for gathering, conversation, and collective experiences that go beyond a simple seat.

Industrial and Prefabricated Production Advantage

The production process of Bast Bench is fully engineered and prefabricated, ensuring high construction precision and proven durability. This approach allows the product to be integrated into projects in the shortest possible time and increases construction efficiency.
