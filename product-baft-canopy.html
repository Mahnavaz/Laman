Woven Canopy

A modular urban structure with minimal design and durable simple materials, creating quality shade for urban spaces. The modular structure enables diverse forms with surface coverage in different dimensions. Beyond shading and visual beauty, it can accommodate a dynamic and public collective space beneath it.

Fusion of Weaving Art and Modern Architecture

Laman Woven Canopy is one of the creative products in the 'For Shade' category, designed with the goal of enhancing the quality of staying and transforming urban open spaces into an environment for presence and tranquility. This structure, with minimal design and durable materials, provides a smart solution for softening the city's visual space.

Material Combination and Weaving Aesthetics

The visual identity of this canopy is based on the contrast between metal hardness and weaving delicacy; the main body of the structure is made of metal, ensuring the product's stability and durability against environmental factors. The canopy roof is formed from a combination of fabric and metal structure, and the use of fabric with a woven mat pattern not only gives the product an attractive and unique structure, but also creates a pleasant atmosphere under the structure by creating a play of light and shadow. The design language of this product is based on simplicity to have minimal visual intervention in the city's appearance.

Flexibility in Dimensions and Form

The structure is fully modular and gives architects freedom in design. One of the key features of this product is the ability to change dimensions according to the specific needs of the project and the area of the desired space. Also, the modular structure provides designers with the possibility of creating diverse forms and covering surfaces in different dimensions.

Social Function and Construction Engineering

The Woven Canopy is more than a physical element; it is a platform for human interactions. Beyond shading functionality, this structure defines a dynamic and enclosed collective space for citizens to pause and interact. The production process of this product is fully engineered and prefabricated, ensuring high precision and speed in execution. Also, by using minimal materials and a modular approach, this product is aligned with sustainable architecture principles.
